Aries Avenir

An amateur, self-taught, time-travelling photographer who delves the depths of a harddrive full of images.

Most of these images are from Saint Seiya: Awakening, which is a game I will never play because I have no idea how. But then I ran out of images. So now Avenir gets to take pictures from wherever I find something decent in my harddrive that doesn't look awful. It was said 'resume / portfolio website' and at no point in that sentence did it say it had to be a tech resume. I always think of art and photography when someone says portfolio. Tech portfolios are on git. But honestly, this is way more fun than trying to do a tech portfolio not on git, so I'm keeping the theme and running with it.

The grid images below all have popovers for captions. Click 'em and see. The carousel above has a ridiculously long scrolltime, because I hate carousels you can't pause with every fiber of my being, and they're required. I want animated stuff when I asked for it. Web design is not one of those places. Stop making stuff move if I can't make it stop. Bah.
